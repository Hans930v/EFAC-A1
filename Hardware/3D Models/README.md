# 3D Models (CAD)

This folder contains the **mechanical design files** for EFAC‑A1.  
It includes all CAD models, assemblies, and printable parts that define the physical mechanism, sensor mounts, and other structural components of the External Feeder‑Assisted Filament Change system.

## Purpose
- Document the mechanical design of EFAC‑A1.  
- Provide CAD references for sensor mounts, feeder mechanisms, and expansion units.  
- Share printable STL/STEP files for prototyping and assembly.  
- Enable reproducibility and community modifications.

## Structure
- **mechanism/** → Core EFAC‑A1 mechanism parts (gears, mounts, brackets).  
- **sensor_mounts/** → CAD files for VL53L0X, Hall sensors, and other electronics integration.  
- **assemblies/** → Full mechanical assemblies and exploded views.  
- **README.md** → This overview file.  

## Accuracy
All models are designed for **functional prototyping**.  
They are validated with 3D prints for dimensional accuracy, but tolerances may vary depending on material (PLA, PETG) and printer calibration.
